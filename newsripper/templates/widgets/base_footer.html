About

News stories ripped straight from the headlines (and their respective news sites).

This tool is for personal consumption of media only. All content stored here is readily accessible without subscription, despite what site owners might say to the contrary.

Content is scraped using BeautifulSoup4, a Python package that can parse HTML documents. These documents are obtained via simple GET request.

Though a target site may use JavaScript to hide content after it has been loaded, we only deal with that original document. So, until they decide not to produce an article at all except to logged-in users, this scaper is totally legit.
